This is Lissa Junqueira, a dentist that has been attending since 2018. She has studied in the Sao Paulo State University - UNESP. Graduated since 2017 and she has been performing surgeons since 2018. She worked in many places and attended many people, providing her an ability to change according to the situation she is in and the kind of person she is attending and giving the patient a more personalized experience. To make her patients the more comfortable possible, she ensures to attend in the best time for the patient and takes as much time as they need, giving them time to ask everything they need and want to know about their treatment so they truly understand what they need to do and why. As a dentist that has special care for the older and the special ones, she has the possibility to attend in the house of those who can't go to her office or is afraid of dentists. This Home Care Attendance is another way to provide health for those in need.
