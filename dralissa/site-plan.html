Content

Home page

She has restored the confidence and happiness of everybody who has passed by her hands. She is a general surgeon that has performed various types of surgery in various types of patients. Her portfolio goes from 7-year-child to 80-year-adult, performing cleanings, extractions, restorations and prothesis. There are additional informations of her work: General surgeon that performs: cleanings, extractions, restorations, canals, permanent and temporary prothesis, partial and total prothesis, orientations, plan treatment, third molar extractions, x-rays, Home Care Attendance, periodical services and so on. Cleanings: Her supper light hands clean everything with efficiency and painlessly. Restoration: With her accurate vision and ability, there is no way to tell the restored teeth from the others. Extractions: You won't even feel the anesthesia! Prothesis: Working with the best prosthetics, she can restore your confidence and happiness restoring your smile in the best way possible. Planning: She will plan every step of your treatment so you now exactly what is going on and why.

About Her

This is Lissa Junqueira, a dentist that has been attending since 2018. She has studied in the Sao Paulo State University - UNESP. Graduated since 2017 and she has been performing surgeons since 2018. She worked in many places and attended many people, providing her an ability to change according to the situation she is in and the kind of person she is attending and giving the patient a more personalized experience. To make her patients the more comfortable possible, she ensures to attend in the best time for the patient and takes as much time as they need, giving them time to ask everything they need and want to know about their treatment so they truly understand what they need to do and why. As a dentist that has special care for the older and the special ones, she has the possibility to attend in the house of those who can't go to her office or is afraid of dentists. This Home Care Attendance is another way to provide health for those in need.
